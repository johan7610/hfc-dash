## Welcome to CoreX

CoreX OS is the operating system for Home Finders Coastal. It's where you manage every part of your real estate business — from the first phone call with a seller, through FICA and mandate signing, all the way to deal registration and commission payout. Every contact, property, document, and deal lives here.

This training pack is organised by role. Find your role below and follow the reading order. Each guide is written as step-by-step instructions with the exact buttons and menu names you'll see on screen.

## What is the "Today" Page?

When you log in to CoreX, you land on **Today** — your personal command centre. It's a smart page that shows you only the things that need your attention right now. It updates every 5 minutes.

You'll see cards like "Today's Appointments", "Overdue Items", "E-Sign Activity", "FICA Tracking", and more. If a card shows zero items, it disappears — so you're never looking at clutter. Think of it as your daily to-do list that writes itself.

Branch managers get extra cards for their team: agent activity, compliance queue, leave approvals, and lost-value alerts.

## Glossary of CoreX Terms

| Term | What it means |
|------|--------------|
| **FFC** | Fidelity Fund Certificate — the annual licence issued by the PPRA that every property practitioner must hold. CoreX tracks your FFC number, certificate upload, and expiry date. |
| **FICA** | Financial Intelligence Centre Act — the law requiring identity verification of clients before transacting. In CoreX, "doing FICA" means sending or uploading identity documents for a contact and having them reviewed by the Compliance Officer. |
| **MDF** | Mandatory Disclosure Form — the legally required document that discloses all known defects about a property. Created through the Documents section. |
| **Mandate** | A signed agreement between a seller and the agency giving authority to market and sell their property. Can be Sole (exclusive to your agency), Open (shared with other agencies), or Dual (shared with one other agency). |
| **PPRA** | Property Practitioners Regulatory Authority — the South African body that regulates all property practitioners. Replaced the old EAAB. |
| **Tier 1 / 2 / 3** | The three levels of compliance complaints. Tier 1 = missing paperwork (seller confirms). Tier 2 = FFC not displayed on advert. Tier 3 = unregistered practitioner (criminal offence). |
| **E-Sign** | Electronic signature — signing a document digitally through a secure link, rather than printing and signing by hand. |
| **Wet Ink** | The traditional way of signing — print the document, sign it with a pen, scan it, and upload it back. Required for sale agreements (OTPs) by law. |
| **Syndication** | Publishing a property listing to external portals like Property24 (P24) or Private Property (PP) so buyers can find it. |
| **Prospecting** | The market intelligence feature that tracks competitor listings from P24 and PP. You can claim leads, match them to buyer requirements, and track price changes. |
| **Core Matches** | The buyer-matching engine. You enter what a buyer is looking for (area, price range, bedrooms, etc.) and CoreX automatically finds matching properties. |
| **Pipeline** | The step-by-step progression of a deal from offer to registration. Each step has a due date and status indicator (green/amber/red). |
| **RAG** | Red/Amber/Green — the traffic-light status system on deal steps. Green = on track, Amber = approaching deadline, Red = overdue. |
| **CO** | Compliance Officer — the person responsible for reviewing and approving FICA submissions, RMCP documents, and compliance complaints. |
| **RMCP** | Risk Management Compliance Programme — the agency's formal compliance manual. Staff must read and acknowledge it. |
| **Go Live** | The moment a property becomes eligible for marketing. All four readiness gates (mandate, FICA, photos, details) must pass first. |

## Where to Get Help

- **Inside CoreX:** Click the help icon (top-right of the sidebar) to submit a feedback report directly to the system team.
- **Ellie AI:** Go to **Tools > Ellie AI** in the sidebar to ask questions about how features work.
- **Your branch manager** is your first point of contact for workflow questions.
- **The Knowledge Base** (Admin > Knowledge Base) contains company policies, templates, and reference documents.
- **System issues:** Report via the Feedback Reports system or contact Johan directly.
