# 03 — Branch Manager Daily Guide

**Who is this for:** Branch Manager
**What you'll learn:** Your daily workflow — oversight, approvals, reports, and team management
**Prerequisites:** You must have a Branch Manager account assigned to a branch.

---

## 1. Your Today Page

Your Today page has everything an agent sees, plus five extra cards for branch management:

| Card | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Branch Agent Watch** | Agents in your branch who may need attention (idle, low activity) |
| **Branch Listings Review** | Properties that need action (stale, expiring mandates, missing documents) |
| **Branch Compliance Queue** | FICA and compliance items awaiting review for your branch |
| **Leave Awaiting Approval** | Staff leave applications waiting for your decision |
| **Branch Lost Value** | Deals or buyers lost recently, with value impact |

Click any card to go directly to that section.

## 2. Manager Oversight

The Oversight page gives you a live feed of items that need attention across your branch.

1. Go to **Dashboard > Oversight** in the sidebar.
2. You'll see a filterable feed of items, grouped by category (overdue tasks, idle properties, missing documents, etc.).
3. Filter by specific agent or category.

### Sending a Nudge
If an agent needs a reminder:
1. Find the item in the Oversight feed.
2. Click **"Nudge"**.
3. Type a short message.
4. Click send — the agent receives an email and an in-app notification.

### Configuring Oversight
Go to **Settings > Oversight** to:
- Set per-category thresholds (e.g., flag properties idle for more than 14 days)
- Enable/disable categories
- Choose notification channels

## 3. Branch Performance and Reports

### Branch Performance
1. Go to **Agency Tracker > Branch > Branch Performance** in the sidebar.
2. See your branch's performance for any month: deal status, income vs budget, agent cards.
3. Click on an agent's name to drill into their individual performance.

### Daily Activity Summary (Branch)
1. Go to **Agency Tracker > Branch > Daily Activity Summary**.
2. See all agents' activity aggregated with drill-down.

### Branch Report (Dashboard)
1. Go to **Dashboard > Branch Report** for conversion funnel, agent leaderboard, and branch-level insights.

### My Agent Dashboard
Go to **Agency Tracker > Branch > My Agent Dashboard** to see your own personal agent metrics (if you also carry a book).

## 4. Approving Deal Steps

When a deal pipeline step requires Branch Manager approval:

1. The agent completes the step and it enters **"Awaiting BM Approval"** status.
2. You'll see a badge on the **Deal Steps Assigned to Me** card on your Today page.
3. Navigate to the deal (via **Admin > Deals > Deal Register** or the Today card link).
4. Open the step that needs approval.
5. Review the attached documents and notes.
6. Click **"Approve"** to advance the deal, or **"Reject"** to send it back with a reason.

## 5. Approving Leave Applications

1. Go to **Leave Management > Applications** in the sidebar (under Branch Manager section).
2. You'll see all pending applications for your branch.
3. Click on an application to review: who's requesting, what type of leave, dates, working days, balance impact, and any branch conflicts.
4. Click **"Approve"** or **"Reject"** (rejection requires a reason, minimum 10 characters).

> **Success:** Approved leave creates a calendar event and notifies the applicant. Rejected leave notifies the applicant with your reason.

## 6. TV Messages

Manage the messages displayed on your office TV screens.

1. Go to **Agency Tracker > Branch > Setup > TV Messages** in the sidebar.
2. You'll see all current messages.
3. Create a new message, edit existing ones, or delete them.
4. To generate a TV display code for a specific screen, use the **TV Code** actions.

---

## 7. Worksheet Market

Set the market data that feeds into your agents' monthly worksheets.

1. Go to **Agency Tracker > Branch > Setup > Worksheet Market**.
2. Enter average sale prices, market conditions, and branch-level defaults.
3. Save — these values populate into agent worksheets for the current period.

---

## 8. Compliance Review

As a BM, you can review compliance items for your branch:

### Verification Queue
1. Go to **Compliance > Verification Queue** to see staff documents awaiting verification (FFC certificates, ID copies, etc.).
2. Click **"Review"** on a document.
3. View the document and either **"Verify Document"** or **"Reject Document"** (with reason).

### FICA Queue
1. Go to **Compliance > FICA** to see submissions for your branch.
2. If you're also the Compliance Officer, you can approve/reject FICA submissions directly (see Guide 07).

### Staff Screening
1. Go to **Compliance > Staff Screening** to manage background screenings for your branch staff.

---

## 9. Targets and Activity Definitions

### Setting Targets
1. Go to **Agency Tracker > Branch > Setup > Daily Activity Targets** or **Agency Tracker > Admin > Targets**.
2. Set daily/monthly targets per agent for sales, listings, and activities.

### Activity Definitions
1. Go to **Agency Tracker > Setup > Activity Definitions**.
2. Define what activities are tracked (calls, viewings, offers, etc.), their weights, and scoring modes.

---

## 10. Sending Reminders

Throughout CoreX, you can send reminders in several places:
- **Oversight > Nudge** — nudge an agent about an overdue item
- **RMCP Dashboard > Send Reminder** — remind staff to acknowledge the RMCP
- **E-Sign Documents > Send Reminder** — remind a signer to complete their signature

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't see Branch Manager section in sidebar | Your role must have the Branch Manager sidebar permission enabled. Ask your admin. |
| Leave application shows "branch conflict" | Another team member is already on leave for the same dates. Review the conflict before approving. |
| Oversight feed is empty | This may mean all items are within threshold. Check your threshold settings. |
| Agent's deal step not showing for approval | Make sure the step's pipeline template has BM approval enabled for that step. |
