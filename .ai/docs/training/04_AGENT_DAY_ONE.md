# 04 — Agent Day One: Your First Hour with CoreX

**Who is this for:** A brand-new agent logging in for the first time
**What you'll learn:** Setting up your account, completing your profile, uploading compliance documents, and finding your way around
**Prerequisites:** You should have received an invitation email from your administrator.

---

## 1. Receiving Your Invitation

Your administrator will send you an email with a secure link to set up your account. The subject line will be something like "Welcome to CoreX — Set Up Your Account".

1. Open the email.
2. Click the link — it takes you to the **Account Setup** page.

> **Important:** This link is unique to you and expires after a set time. If it's expired, ask your admin to click **"Resend Invite"** on your user record.

[SCREENSHOT: The invitation email with the setup link highlighted]

---

## 2. Setting Up Your Account

1. On the Account Setup page, enter your new password (at least 8 characters).
2. Confirm the password by typing it again.
3. Click **"Set Password"**.
4. You'll be redirected to the login page.

[SCREENSHOT: The Account Setup page with password fields]

---

## 3. First Login

1. Enter your email and the password you just created.
2. Click **"Log In"**.
3. You land on the **Today** page — your daily command centre.

[SCREENSHOT: The Today page as seen by a new agent with action cards]

## 4. My Portal — Your Home Base

Your first stop should be **My Portal**. Click it in the left sidebar.

[SCREENSHOT: My Portal sidebar item, possibly with amber attention dot]

My Portal has several sections:

### Profile
- Your name, email, phone, and personal details
- Click **"Edit"** to update your information

### Compliance Documents
This is where you upload the documents your agency needs on file:
- **FFC Certificate** — your Fidelity Fund Certificate from the PPRA
- **ID Copy** — certified copy of your identity document
- **Proof of Address** — utility bill or bank statement (less than 2 months old)
- **Tax Clearance** — SARS tax clearance certificate
- **Qualifications** — your NQF/education certificates
- **PI Insurance** — professional indemnity insurance (if applicable)

**To upload a document:**
1. Click the upload area for the document type.
2. Select your file.
3. Click **"Upload"**.

> **What happens next:** Your uploaded documents go to the **Verification Queue** where the Compliance Officer will review and verify them. You'll see the status change from "Pending" to "Verified" once approved. If rejected, you'll get a notification explaining what to fix.

### Payslips
Once you start receiving commissions, your payslips appear here.

### Leave
Apply for leave, view your leave balance, and track your applications.

### FICA Tracking
See the status of any FICA submissions linked to your name.

## 5. Completing Required Training

If your agency has set up required training courses, you'll see an amber dot on the **Training** menu item in the sidebar.

1. Click **Training** in the sidebar (under the Agents section).
2. You'll see a list of courses. Required courses are marked.
3. Click on a course to view its lessons.
4. For each lesson:
   - Click **"Start"** to begin.
   - Read/watch the content.
   - Click **"Complete"** when finished.
5. After completing all lessons, click **"Acknowledge"** on the course.

> **Success:** The amber dot disappears from the sidebar once all required courses are complete.

## 6. Understanding Your Today Page

Your Today page shows up to 19 different cards, but only the ones that apply to you right now. Here's what the most common ones mean:

| Card | What it means | What to do |
|------|--------------|------------|
| **Today's Appointments** | Your calendar events for today | Click to see details, prepare for meetings |
| **Overdue Items** | Tasks or events past their due date | Action them or reschedule |
| **Listings Needing Attention** | Properties that need updates (stale, expiring) | Visit the property and update |
| **E-Sign Activity** | Documents waiting for your signature or awaiting others | Sign or send reminders |
| **My FICA Tracking** | FICA requests you've sent and their status | Follow up on pending ones |
| **My Compliance** | Your personal compliance status (FFC, training) | Upload missing documents |
| **Training & Qualifications** | Courses you need to complete | Go to Training and finish them |
| **Recent Activity** | Summary of what you've done recently | For your reference |

The page refreshes automatically every 5 minutes.

## 7. Finding Your Way Around the Sidebar

The left sidebar is your navigation. It's organised into expandable groups:

| Section | What's inside |
|---------|--------------|
| **Dashboard** | Today, Calendar, Tasks, Performance, Buyer Pipeline |
| **My Portal** | Your profile, documents, payslips, leave |
| **Real Estate** | Properties, Contacts, Core Matches, Prospecting, Presentations |
| **Agency Documents** | Read-only company documents |
| **My Earnings** | Your commission dashboard |
| **Revenue Share** | Revenue share calculator |
| **Training** | Learning courses |
| **Agency Tracker** | Worksheet, Listings, Daily Activity, Deals |
| **Documents** | Create and manage documents, e-sign |
| **Rentals** | Rental management |
| **Compliance** | FICA, RMCP, compliance reporting |
| **Tools** | Ellie AI, calculators, PDF tools, filing register |

Click any group name to expand it and see the items inside.

## 8. Key Daily Habits

Once you're set up, here's what a typical day looks like:

1. **Start with Today** — check your action cards
2. **Check Calendar** — review today's appointments
3. **Log Daily Activity** — go to Agency Tracker > My Daily Activity and record your calls, viewings, etc.
4. **Update Properties** — check for any properties needing attention
5. **Check E-Sign Documents** — send reminders for overdue signatures
6. **End of day** — save your daily activity, check Tomorrow's appointments

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Invitation link says "expired" or "invalid" | Ask your admin to click **"Resend Invite"** on your user record. |
| Can't log in after setting password | Double-check your email spelling. Try **"Forgot your password?"** on the login page. |
| My Portal shows amber dot but I can't find what's missing | Check: is your FFC uploaded? Are all required training courses done? |
| Document shows "Rejected" | Read the rejection reason, fix the issue, and re-upload. |
| Can't see some sidebar items | Your role determines what you can access. Talk to your branch manager if something seems missing. |
